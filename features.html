Features

Create An Account

To begin, sign up for an account. Provide your information to set up your profile, and you're ready to begin using the application! Once registered, you can access your account anytime by logging in with your username and password.

Add Your Academic History

Once you've created an account, you can add your academic history by clicking the plus button at the top of the page. A pop-up will appear, prompting you to enter the course name. You can scroll through the list or use the search function to filter for it. Once you've selected the course, click "Add Course." Repeat this process for the remaining courses you have taken. Finally, click the "Done" button at the bottom of the screen to complete the process.

View Suggested Courses

After creating an account and adding your academic history, visit the dashboard to view suggested courses. You'll find recommendations for Monday, Wednesday, and Friday classes, as well as for Tuesday and Thursday classes. Additionally, you'll see alternative options and explanations for any unavailable courses.

Edit Your Profile Page

To update your profile details, access the profile icon located at the top right corner of the dashboard. Enter your current and new password to adjust the number of courses and preferred difficulty level. Once done, click update, back to main page, log out and log back in to apply the changes.
